Why ELK?

Impermanent Loss Protection (ILP)

Prevents users from being penalised for providing liquidity.

Each day in a farm provides liquidity providers with ~2.5% of ILP coverage until 100% has been reached. Users can claim ELK rewards at any time; ILP coverage is prorated based on length of time in the farm.

Read More

Dynamic farming

Promotes a sustainable token trajectory.

Emission targets are used to adjust rewards dynamically on each chain at the start of each farming round.
